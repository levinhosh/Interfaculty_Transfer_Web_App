RE: INTER- FACULTY / PROGRAMME TRANSFERS

I take this opportunity to welcome you and congratulate you for successfully enrolling yourselves to Chuka University. The process of transferring to other programmes/courses will begin on Wednesday 14 th September, 2020 and end on Tuesday 20 th September, 2020.

Transfer strictly depends on the following:

i. Meeting the last year cut-off points for KUCCPS in the cluster subjects and University.

ii. Whether there is a vacancy in the programme you want to transfer to. Procedure:

iii. Students will collect programme transfer forms from their current faculties.

iv. Students will fill the forms and attach copies of result slips and return to their current faculties.

v. Faculties will summarize in Microsoft excel the information for the two choices given. The summary will indicate the students’ details and the cut-off points of the programme the student intends to transfer to. Two columns will be left for the weighted cluster points the student attained for the new programme and remarks.

vi. Summaries of both soft and hard copies are to be submitted to the office of the Registrar (AA) not later than Friday 23 rd September, 2016.

vii. The office of the Registrar( AA) will communicate to all successful and unsuccessful applicants 6. Meanwhile, all students are advised to continue with the programmes they were admitted in until it is communicated by the office of the Registrar Academic Affairs.

 

Successful applicants must report to their new faculties for the amendment of their records failure to which one will not be considered to have changed to the new course.
